About Heard

Heard isn't a chatbot. It won't solve your problems, offer productivity hacks, or flood you with advice.

You write a message. Just a sentence. You choose a tone. You decide how expressive you want the response to be. Then Heard responds, once, with a voice of your choice.

That's it. No conversation threads, no pressure. Just one flicker of recognition. A pause. A moment to feel heard.
